Gilbert Coin Dispenser

In Science class, Me and my team were assined a project about making an Anemometer, my teammates made the desing of the concept, of how it will spin, what motor will use, shape, size, etc. that day i came home and desinged and made a prototype on a bread board and putted it all in a cardboard prototype of a future desing.We used a 5v dc motor, and 128*64 oled screen, and an arduino nano. I learned about teamwork, programming, electronics, and motor controling. This project was a fun and educational experience that helped me learn more about wind and motors power that it sends back from spining.
